# StackWave Investor Pitch Deck

## Problem
Web3 organizations struggle with fragmented governance tools and a lack of actionable analytics to understand their token holders.

## Solution
StackWave provides a unified platform combining DAO voting, staking rewards, and AI-powered wallet intelligence.

## Market Size
The DAO and DeFi sectors are multi-billion dollar markets with rapid growth in institutional interest.

## Business Model
- **SaaS Subscription:** For organizations needing advanced analytics.
- **Transaction Fees:** Small percentage on staking rewards or treasury management.

## Competitive Landscape
StackWave differentiates itself by integrating AI analytics directly into the governance workflow, providing a "moat" of data intelligence.
